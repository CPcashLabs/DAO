## Role summary

A Web3 Community Business Expert stands between community and ecosystem growth. This role connects value propositions with partnership opportunities and helps turn community momentum into real ecosystem expansion.

## What you will focus on

- Identify and advance external collaboration opportunities
- Design growth, activation, and partnership plans aligned with the project vision
- Connect with potential partners across communities, brands, and Web3 projects
- Bridge user feedback, community needs, and business direction
- Push strategic conversations into execution and long-term collaboration

## What we hope you bring

- Strong understanding of the Web3 ecosystem, community dynamics, and business collaboration
- Experience in community growth, BD, partnerships, or ecosystem operations
- Clear communication, strong follow-through, and resource coordination ability
- Alignment with privacy and decentralization as long-term values

## Why this role matters

Living decentralized ecosystems depend not only on technology, but also on people who understand communities, relationships, and how real collaboration happens.

If you want to combine industry insight, business judgment, and shared values, this role is an important place to do it.
