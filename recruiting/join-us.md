## What kind of participation we welcome

We welcome volunteer contributors, long-term collaborators, and people who want to start with a conversation before finding the right way to contribute.

## Helpful information to prepare

When you reach out, it helps to include:

- The role or direction you are interested in
- Why you believe in a privacy-first and decentralized Web3 world
- Projects, communities, or work you have participated in before
- How you hope to contribute

## What matters most to us

We do not need a highly polished traditional resume. We care more about:

- Your real motivation and judgment
- Your consistency and ability to act
- Your willingness to build with the community over time

## Recommended reading before you reach out

If you want the broader context before contacting us, we recommend reading:

- [Vision]({{ '/roadmap/vision/' | relative_url }}): the values behind CPcashLabs DAO
- [Roadmap Whitepaper]({{ '/roadmap/whitepaper/' | relative_url }}): the long-term direction and development phases

These pages help explain how the roles in the recruiting module connect to the larger direction of the ecosystem.
