## Role summary

Community volunteers help create warmth, order, and connection. You will support newcomers, strengthen conversations, and help the community become a place where people genuinely want to stay and contribute.

## What you will do

- Welcome new members and help them understand the community direction
- Support a friendly, clear, and constructive discussion atmosphere
- Assist with activities, content sharing, topic summaries, and daily operations
- Identify active members and encourage deeper participation
- Collect front-line feedback to help the team improve

## What we hope you bring

- Genuine interest in Web3, privacy, and decentralization
- Willingness to communicate and connect with different kinds of people
- Reliability and the ability to participate consistently
- Basic writing, coordination, or community support skills

## Who this role is for

This role fits people who want to enter Web3 ecosystem building from the front line of community work and grow through real collaboration.

## What you can gain

- A deeper understanding of how decentralized communities operate
- Practical experience in community operations and coordination
- Stronger connections with people who care about privacy and the open web
- A clearer long-term place for yourself in ecosystem building
