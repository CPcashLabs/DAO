## The current execution module

Recruiting is the current action-focused module of CPcashLabs DAO. It exists to bring the right people into the ecosystem and turn belief in decentralization into real community capacity.

## What this module contains

- [Community Volunteer]({{ '/recruiting/community-volunteer/' | relative_url }}): for community support, connection, and activation
- [Web3 Community Business Expert]({{ '/recruiting/web3-community-business-expert/' | relative_url }}): for ecosystem growth, collaboration, and business development
- [How to Join]({{ '/recruiting/join-us/' | relative_url }}): guidance for applicants and contributors

## Why this module matters now

Before a DAO can scale, it needs people who can communicate, organize, recruit, and build trust across the ecosystem.

That is why recruiting is one of the first major modules in this documentation site.
