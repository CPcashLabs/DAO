## What is CPcashLabs DAO

The CPcashLabs DAO project is a multilingual documentation entry point for the **CPcashLabs brand ecosystem**, designed to introduce our values, ecosystem direction, and community building opportunities.

At the current stage, the site is mainly focused on **recruiting, community building, and ecosystem collaboration**, while keeping the broader CPcashLabs ecosystem narrative visible.

## What kind of ecosystem we are building

Through the CPcashLabs brand, we aim to build a Web3 ecosystem rooted in community consensus, privacy protection, and decentralized collaboration:

- Users should own their identity, data, and assets
- Communities should be able to co-create, participate, and govern
- Value exchange should be transparent, open, and verifiable
- Long-term contributors should be able to gather around a shared ideal

## Current focus

The current content focuses on two core directions:

- Introducing the [CPcashLabs Ecosystem]({{ '/ecosystem/' | relative_url }}) and our [Vision]({{ '/vision/' | relative_url }})
